Checkout Failover Runbook

When checkout error rates spike after a database failover:

1. Confirm whether payments-db promoted a new primary.
2. Check whether checkout-api is still targeting the old primary host.
3. Restart checkout-api if the service did not refresh the primary target.
4. Confirm 5xx rate returns to baseline.
5. Create a follow-up ticket if customer impact exceeded 15 minutes.

Known failure pattern:

- checkout-api may keep a stale primary target after failover if the service was already connected during promotion.

Expected symptom:

- elevated 502 or upstream connection failures on checkout-api
